What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device that allow websites to recognize you and retain certain information about your visit.

We use cookies to track your usage patterns, improve user experience, and ensure the functionality of the speed test. These cookies help us understand how our site is being used and enable us to make improvements.
