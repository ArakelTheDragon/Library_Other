Testing Method

Our speed test measures your internet download speed by fetching test files from various servers. The test follows these steps:

  • Test files ranging from 1MB to 1.5GB are downloaded dynamically based on speed.
  • The number of parallel downloads increases from 2 to 8 for more accuracy.
  • The test runs for 10 seconds per step, checking for stable results.
  • Speed is measured in Mbps (megabits per second), and results are updated in real-time.
