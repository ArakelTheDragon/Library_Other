About CfCbazar

CfCbazar stands for "CfC Bazar" — a name that reflects our mission: helping people discover smart, creative, and affordable ideas, tools, services, and content that are worth every cent.

The term "Bazar" is the Czech spelling of "bazaar." We chose it intentionally — it’s unique, recognizable, and easy to promote online while honoring our roots.

What We Offer

CfCbazar is a growing platform combining innovation, creativity, and practical value. We bring together:

  • Smart Deals: Up to 80% discounts on curated tech, lifestyle, and digital products
  • DIY Hacks: Electronics, software, and engineering tutorials
  • Games: A mix of free browser-based and premium games
  • Music & Media: CfC Music TV and other creative content
  • WorkToken: A crypto-based project redefining fair labor value worldwide

Our Mission

We believe clever ideas and fair value should be available to everyone — no matter where they live or what they earn. Our goal is to empower people to do more, create more, and live better... for cents.

Whether you're looking for a deal, learning something new, or exploring ways to earn and contribute, CfCbazar is your gateway to smarter living powered by community-driven innovation.

Join the Movement

Follow us on social media, explore our features, or support the mission by trying out the WorkToken. CfCbazar is more than a marketplace — it’s a movement built around fairness, fun, and future thinking.
