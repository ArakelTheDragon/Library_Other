Download the friles from:
https://github.com/martin-ger/esp_wifi_repeater/tree/master

Or use the esp_wifi_repeater-master folder in this git repo.

Extract the files and open the firmware folder

Upload the files to the nodeMUC with https://esp.huhn.me/ which works in Chrome/Edge but not in Firefox.
0x00000.bin at 00000
0x02000.bin at 02000
0x82000.bin at 0x82000

Power off and power on the NodeMCU ESP8266. You might need to do this and press the reset button multiple times, sometimes to wait as well 5 min after flashing.
Connect to the MyApp WiFi
Visit 192.168.4.1

Enter the SSID to repat and PW. Use the mesh option on the top. Wait 2 min.
Check the serial terminal on https://esp.huhn.me/ to see if the mesh is successfull.
